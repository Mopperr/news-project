Water Baptism by Full Immersion

After accepting Yeshua, the next step in your faith journey is water baptism. Baptism is an outward expression of your inward faith‚Äîa public declaration of your new life in Messiah.

What is Baptism?

Baptism means to be fully immersed in water, symbolizing:

  • Death to your old life: Going under the water represents dying to sin
  • Burial: Being submerged represents being buried with Christ
  • Resurrection: Coming up from the water represents rising to new life in Yeshua
"Therefore we were buried with Him through baptism into death, that just as Christ was raised from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life." ‚Äî Romans 6:4
"Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." ‚Äî Matthew 28:19

Why Full Immersion?

The Greek word "baptizo" means to immerse or submerge. Full immersion baptism follows the biblical example set by Yeshua Himself when He was baptized in the Jordan River (Matthew 3:16). The early church practiced baptism by full immersion, as it best represents the complete transformation of becoming a new creation in Christ.

"When He had been baptized, Jesus came up immediately from the water; and behold, the heavens were opened to Him, and He saw the Spirit of God descending like a dove and alighting upon Him." ‚Äî Matthew 3:16

Ready to be baptized? Contact a local Bible-believing congregation or reach out to Vision For Israel to learn about baptism opportunities in your area.
