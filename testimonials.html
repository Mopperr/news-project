Voices from the Community

Michal Levi & Avital Shtein

Welfare Department, Jerusalem Municipality

"We would like to thank you for your generous donation to the asylum seekers' children entering the first grade. There are about 3000 asylum seekers living in Jerusalem who face a difficult reality of life and economic distress. The children and parents were excited to receive the beautiful and high-quality schoolbags. We want to thank you for your willingness to help people who often feel 'transparent' and invisible."
